Who I am

Former Law student turned Front-end Developer

Hello! I'm Imani, a front-end developer based in Montenegro. I enjoy creating web layouts, data visulaizations and clean UI applications. I graduated university in 2018 with a degree in political science, with the intention of going to law school in the Bay Area. It was during my first year in lawschool that I decided to make the career transition to tech. After completing my first year in saying, 'I have a prediction about the world that no one knows yet, because this gave me insight. Let's go test that prediction,' and have the prediction be correct. I can't tell you how many people say they were turned off from science because of a science teacher that completely sucked out all the inspiration and enthusiasm they had for the course.

We have bred multiple generations of people who have not experienced knowing where you are the moment a news story broke, with that news story being great and grand and something that elevates society instead of diminishes it.
